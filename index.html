RECIPE

  • 1

    Preheat your oven according to the pizza dough instructions or to around 450°F (230°C).

  • 2

    Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness and shape on a floured surface.

  • 3

    Place the rolled dough onto a pizza stone, baking sheet, or pizza pan.

  • 4

    Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

  • 5

    Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.

  • 6

    Add another layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top to hold everything together.

  • 7

    Carefully transfer the pizza into the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  • 8

    Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and sprinkle chopped fresh cilantro leaves over the top.

  • 9

    Allow the pizza to cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade Peppy Paneer Pizza!
